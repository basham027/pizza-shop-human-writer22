    Introduction

    The interior design process can be broken down into five stages: ideation, research, planning, execution, and evaluation. In ideation, designers come up with ideas for a project. In research, they look into existing designs and trends to find inspiration. In planning, they create a detailed plan of what they want the project to look like. In execution, they work on designing and creating the project. Finally, in evaluation, they look back on the project and assess how well it turned out.
