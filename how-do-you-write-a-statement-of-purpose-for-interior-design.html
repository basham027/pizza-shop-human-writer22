    How do you write a statement of purpose for interior design?

    When writing a statement of purpose for interior design, it is important to be clear and concise. The statement of purpose should outline the design goals of the business and what the designer intends to achieve.     A statement of purpose for interior design is to create a space that is functional, beautiful, and comfortable.
